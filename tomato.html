Agridera Fresh Market Orange Hybrids

*****

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining disease resistances with high quality traits adapted to different markets globally.

Our breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse, Open-field Indeterminate, and Bush Type Varieties.

Greenhouse Breeding Program

Aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. Focus on yield and resistance.

Open-field Varieties

Focus on large fruits (200 grams plus) and soil borne disease resistance.

Specialty Breeding

We breed unique shapes and sizes for diverse Mediterranean markets.
