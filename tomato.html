Agridera Fresh Market Orange Hybrids

*****

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of citrus hybrids. We invest intensively in research and development to provide high-quality solutions to growers and consumers globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining disease resistances with superior Brix levels and fruit quality traits adapted to different markets around the world.

Our breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Early Season Navels, Mid-Season Universal Hybrids, and Late-Season Juice Varieties.

Early Navel Program

Aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. Focus on easy-peeling traits, large fruit size, and resistance to powdery mildew.

Universal Hybrids

Focus on high sugar content (Brix), large round fruits (250g+), and soil-borne disease resistance like Phytophthora.

Juice Varieties

We breed high-yield juice types with thin skin and standard sizes for the Mediterranean processing industry.
