Agridera Fresh Market Orange Hybrids

*****

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of citrus seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining Citrus Tristeza Virus (CTV) and Citrus Stubborn Disease resistances with other soil-borne diseases into superior orange breeding lines and hybrids. Moreover, comprehensive efforts are being undertaken to develop varieties resistance to Mal Secco and other fungal pathogens.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding is for earliness, different fruit sizes, rind thickness, deep orange color, flavor, seedlessness, shelf life, and high juice content. Combinations of quality features and disease resistance are sought in a variety of types, namely navel, common, and pigmented oranges.

Agridera Orange breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Early Season Navels, Mid-Season Universal Hybrids, and Late-Season Juice Varieties.
Early Navel
Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. For early harvest, easy peeling, large fruit size and resistances to common citrus viruses. Some hybrids may include more resistances, such as cold tolerance.

Universal
Hybrids

This breeding is for Moroccan, Egyptian, and Brazilian markets. The main focus is on large round shape fruits (220 grams plus); high Brix levels and resistance to Phytophthora and CTV. In the near future, F3 selections will be included.

Juice & Industrial
Varieties

This breeding is for Mediterranean processing markets. We breed round and oval shapes, standard sizes (160-200 grams), and high-yield juice types (min 50% juice content). We combine the resistances to Nematodes as basic resistances.

Agridera operates through international and local distributors around the world.
