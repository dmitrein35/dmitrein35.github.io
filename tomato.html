Agridera Fresh Market Tomato Hybrids

*****

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of tomato seeds. Agridera's professional team of breeders has many years of experience and is utilizing the latest technologies in the market.

The company invests a large part of its turnover in Research and Development in order to satisfy the requirements of its clients and provide them with the best seeds available.

Our goal is to produce and provide varieties with high yields and high resistance to diseases combined with the best quality of the fruit in terms of shelf life, color, and taste.

Greenhouse Tomato Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. The goal is to provide a long shelf life variety (LSL) with a big fruit (200-250 grams), deep red color, and high resistance to TYLCV.

Open-field Indeterminate Tomatoes

This breeding is for Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The goal is to provide a high yield variety, with high quality of fruit and resistance to TSWV, TYLCV, and Mi.

Open-field Bush Type Tomatoes

This breeding is for Mediterranean markets. The goal is to provide varieties with high yield and high quality of fruit for both standard round fruit and Roma type.
